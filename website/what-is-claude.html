What is Claude?

Claude is an AI assistant created by a company called Anthropic. You type a message to it — just like texting a friend — and it writes back. It understands plain English, so you never need to use special commands or code.

What can you use it for?

  • Answering questions on any topic
  • Helping you write emails, essays, or social posts
  • Explaining things in simple terms
  • Helping you think through a decision
  • Writing or explaining code
  • Summarising long documents
  • Brainstorming ideas

Is it like Google?

Not quite. Google finds web pages for you to read. Claude actually reads, understands, and responds. You can have a back-and-forth conversation with it, ask follow-up questions, and say things like "explain that more simply" — and it will.

Is it safe to use?

Claude is designed to be helpful, harmless, and honest. It won't help with harmful requests, and it will tell you when it's unsure about something rather than making things up. That said, always double-check important facts — no AI is perfect.

Do I need to pay?

Claude has a free tier at claude.ai that is more than enough to get started. A paid plan exists if you want faster responses or heavier use, but it is not required.
