Getting Started

Step 2 of 7

You can be up and running with Claude in under 5 minutes.

Step 1: Create a free account

  1. Go to claude.ai in your browser
  2. Click Sign up
  3. Enter your email and create a password
  4. Check your email and click the confirmation link

Step 2: Start a new conversation

  1. Once logged in, you'll see a text box at the bottom of the screen
  2. Click on it and type your first message
  3. Press Enter or click the send button

Your first message — make it good

The way you phrase your first message matters. Here's the difference between a vague message and a useful one:

Too vague

"Help me with my email."

Much better

"I need to write a professional email to my landlord asking to fix a leaking tap. Can you help me draft it? Keep it polite and brief."

The second message gives Claude context: who you're writing to, what you need, and how you want it. You'll learn more about this in the next section.

Easy first messages to try

  • "Explain what you are in simple terms"
  • "What can you help me with?"
  • "I'm new to using AI. Can you show me something useful you can do?"
  • "Help me write a short bio about myself. I work in [your field] and I [one thing about you]."

Things to know from the start

  • Claude remembers your conversation — you can ask follow-up questions and it knows what you were talking about
  • You can ask it to try again — if an answer isn't right, just say "that's not quite what I meant, can you try again?"
  • Starting over is fine — use the "New conversation" button on the left if you want a fresh start on a different topic
  • There's no wrong way — experiment freely. Claude won't judge you.

When to start a new conversation

Claude works best when a conversation stays on one topic. If you've been asking about recipes and suddenly want help with a work email, start a new conversation. Mixing topics can confuse things.
