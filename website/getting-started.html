Getting Started

You can be up and running with Claude in under 5 minutes. Here's how.

Step 1: Create a free account

  1. Go to claude.ai in your browser
  2. Click Sign up
  3. Enter your email address and create a password
  4. Check your email and click the confirmation link

Step 2: Start a new conversation

  1. Once logged in, you'll see a text box at the bottom of the screen
  2. Click on it and type your first message
  3. Press Enter or click the send button

Step 3: Write your first message

Don't overthink it — just type naturally. Here are some easy first messages to try:

  • "Explain what you are in simple terms"
  • "What can you help me with?"
  • "Write a short introduction about me: I am [your name] and I work in [your field]"

Tips for your first conversation

  • You can ask follow-up questions — Claude remembers the conversation
  • If you don't like the answer, say "can you try again more simply"
  • You can start a new conversation at any time using the button on the left
  • There's no wrong way to use it — experiment freely
