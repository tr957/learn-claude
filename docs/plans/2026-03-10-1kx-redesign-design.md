# 1kx Website Redesign — Design Doc

**Date:** 2026-03-10

## Purpose

Reposition the site from a generic Claude tutorial into a practical "how to use Claude at 1kx" guide for investment, research, operations, portfolio support, and internal comms workflows.

## Audience

- Smart, non-technical people at 1kx
- New joiners or teammates wanting to use Claude more effectively
- Three role tracks: Investment & Research / Operations & Comms / Platform & Portfolio

## Navigation

- Main nav: site name + all 9 pages (abbreviated)
- Role nav row on every page: Investment & Research | Operations & Comms | Platform & Portfolio
- "Next step →" button at the bottom of every page

## Content principles

- Each workflow section: When to use / What to paste in / Copyable prompt / Better version
- Heavy use of copyable fill-in-the-blank prompts throughout
- Professional tone, no jargon, concrete examples
- Safe use section covers: what not to paste, checking outputs, judgment vs automation
